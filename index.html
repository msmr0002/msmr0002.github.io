Bio

Hello and thank you for visiting my website! My name is Yuji Masumura, and I am a PhD studnet in the Department of Government at the University of Texas at Austin. My major is international security with a focus of East Asia. Specifically, my research is about military alliance, the UN Security Council, and international status. Methodologically, I mainly use game theory and statistics, and sometimes survey experiments. Please see my research page for more details.

I am from Kyoto, Japan. I earned a LL.B in 2020 from Hitotsubashi University in Tokyo, Japan. You can view my CV here (Updataed June 2023).
