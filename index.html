Bio

Thank you for visiting my website! My name is Yuji Masumura, and I am an Associate Professor in the Graduate School of Law at Kobe University in Japan. I received a Ph.D. in Government from the University of Texas at Austin in 2025. My research interest concerns the formal and quantitative study of international security with a focus on East Asia. Please see my research page for more details.

I am from Kyoto, Japan. I earned an LL.B. in 2020 from Hitotsubashi University in Tokyo, Japan. You can view my CV here (Updated July 2025).
